How much can you invest?
  • Simple interest: We currently accept minimum of 50,000 naira and maximum of 5 million naira.

How long is the term of my investment?

How is interest paid?

  • Compound interest - Get paid the cumulative ROI derived from your investment at the end of your investment term.
Please Note:
  • Simple interest is paid on monthly basis till the end of term.
  • Compound interest is paid only at the end of term.
  • Your principal will be paid at the end of term.
